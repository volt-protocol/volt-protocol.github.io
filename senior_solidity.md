Senior Solidity Engineer

Volt Protocol is a lending protocol with a native stablecoin, VOLT. Unlike most stablecoins, VOLT is not pegged to any existing currency. Instead, it is stable in regards to the consumer price index (CPI), meaning that holders of VOLT have their purchasing power protected against inflation. Depositors mint VOLT and borrow against their long tail cryptoassets including derivatives like wrapped staked Ether (WSTETH) and liquidity pool shares. Holders maintain a stable purchasing power.

Our seed round supporters include expert contributors in decentralized finance (incl. founders of Rari, Fei, Frax, Tokemak, Primitive Finance) and DeFi native funds (seed round led jointly by Framework Ventures and Nascent).

Our v1, which we plan to launch late Q12022, is the same code as RAI. The main difference at first is we will support additional collateral types, such as ETH staking derivatives and liquidity pool shares.

Going forward, we believe DeFi will scale to support an even greater diversity of instruments and lending terms than exist in the traditional financial world. To enable as much of this diversity as possible to exist in a single lending system without liquidity fragmentation, some of our priorities for improvement are:
* issuance (such as using Fei Turbo to issue VOLT)
* optimistic collateral onboarding
* oracles (move to use purely on chain/trustless source of truth over time)
* Layer 2 -- designing a safe mechanism to allow VOLT issuance and redemption on trustless L2s
* dynamic parameter tuning. Figuring out ways to set more system parameters trustlessly using on chain data, for example basing stability fees on implied vol from on chain derivatives markets in the future
